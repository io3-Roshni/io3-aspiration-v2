Q. What kind of precautions should I follow during the trip?

A. There are 5 rules to avoid Coronavirus. You should follow and apply this to your loved ones during the trip.

  1. Always wear a mask in a crowded place.
  2. Wash your hands from both sides with good quality handwash certified by medical professionals.
  3. Carry a sanitizer with you after touching anything then sanitize your hands properly.
  4. Maintain at least one-meter distance with everybody if not possible then leave that place immediately and search for the safe place.
  5. If you and your tripmates like friends, or a family member feel something awkward then immediately call for medical help and get checked.

Q. How to get medical help immediately?

A. You can call 1075 immediately without any delay and if not possible then inform your trip leader instantly. If you feel uncomfortable then do not start your trip because in between the trip, at some places it will be very hard to reach for medical help.

Q. What are the symptoms of Coronavirus?

A. The Coronavirus (COVID-19) symptoms look like normal but do not take the risk. The COVID-19 symptoms are:

  • Fever
  • Cough
  • Continuously Sneezing & running nose
  • Tiredness
  • Breathing Difficulty.

If you are feeling any of the symptoms or all then without any delay isolate yourself from the others and call 1075 COVID-19 emergency number. This number is activated by the Union Health ministry. You can also contact them by dropping an e-mail at COVID-19 e-mail helpline: ncov2019@gmail.com.
